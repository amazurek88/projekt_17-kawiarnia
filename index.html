what's make our coffee special!

The coffee beans available in our offer differ not only in aroma and taste, but also in intensity. By deciding on the degree of grinding, the customer partially decides what effect will be in his cup and on the palate, but a large part of these experiences is already contained inside the beans that we have in our assortment. Various methods of cultivation, drying, roasting, maturing in various climatic conditions, as well as auxiliaries or processing methods, determine the quality of a given coffee plant.
